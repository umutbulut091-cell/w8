Diversity encompasses various aspects, including race, ethnicity, gender, socioeconomic status, and learning abilities. By recognizing and valuing these differences, educational institutions can create inclusive spaces that promote understanding, empathy, and respect. Furthermore, inclusive environments often lead to higher academic achievement as students feel more valued and supported.
